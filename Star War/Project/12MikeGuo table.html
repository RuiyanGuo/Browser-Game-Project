Operating instructions

  • W--move the plane up
  • S--move the plane down
  • A--move the plane left
  • D--move the plane right
  • L--activate firing mechanism
Notice
  • To start, click the pink window first and press enter
  • To restart, refresh the webpage
  • The colorful objects could be shooted
  • The black objects couldn not be shooted
  • For the black objects, you can only dodge them
  • The number on the top is your score
  • *DO NOT let the objects touch the red sphere on the aircraft, otherwise the game would be over
